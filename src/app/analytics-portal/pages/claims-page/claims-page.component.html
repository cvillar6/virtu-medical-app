Claims Module

The Claims Module provides a clear and detailed picture of patient costs, facilitating thorough financial analysis. It offers visualizations of total claim costs by month, expense type, payor, and per member per month (PMPM).

The module also includes detailed member information for each submitted claim, helping healthcare providers make informed financial decisions.

Main benefits

  • • Perform Comprehensive Cost Analysis: Analyze and compare patient costs across various health plans to optimize financial outcomes.

  • • Leverage Financial Decision Support: Use detailed claims data to make informed financial decisions and enhance overall financial management.

  • • Achieve Time Savings: Consolidate all claims data in one centralized location, reducing the need for extensive document searches and streamlining workflows.
