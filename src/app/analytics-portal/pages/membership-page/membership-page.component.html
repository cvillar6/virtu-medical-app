Membership module

The Membership Module in Virtu’s Analytics Portal offers complete control over patient tracking and engagement. It tracks new, active, disenrolled, and transfer patients, helping you monitor membership variance and trends over time.

With seamless EHR connectivity, this module allows you to gauge patient engagement by tracking how frequently patients visit their primary care providers (PCPs) and the average time it takes for patients to see their PCP after enrolling in a new health plan.

The module also includes dashboards for identifying orphan patients and those outside your service area, providing high-level analysis with the ability to drill down to individual patient data.

Main benefits

  • • Enhanced Patient Engagement: Identify and track the engagement of patients, helping to reduce disenrollment and inactivity.

  • • Comprehensive Membership Insights: Access detailed information about patient locations across lines of business, health plans, and medical centers.

  • • Improved Patient Care: Utilize insights to improve the frequency and efficiency of patient visits.

Key features
