Revenue Module

The Revenue Module provides a detailed overview of your organization’s total revenue, segmented by line of business (LOB), by month, and per member per month (PMPM).

It includes visualizations and detailed membership information at the patient level, helping you gain a comprehensive understanding of your financial performance and trends.

  • • Drive Data-Driven Decision-Making: Use detailed financial insights to make informed projections and decisions.
