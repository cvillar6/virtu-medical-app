MRA module

The MRA Module offers a comprehensive dashboard that provides an overview of MRA scores, broken down by actual scores, new and disenrolled patients, and the top 10 scores by primary care provider (PCP).

It also includes visualizations and details of MRA scores segmented by health plans, medical centers, and PCPs, enabling you to track trends and identify areas for improvement in patient care.

Main benefits

  • • Improved Risk Management: Tracks and monitors MRA scores to identify gaps in patient care.

  • • Enhanced Clinical Documentation: Provides insights to help close gaps in care and improve clinical documentation.

  • • Better Patient Outcomes: Monitors patient scores and trends to drive better health outcomes.
