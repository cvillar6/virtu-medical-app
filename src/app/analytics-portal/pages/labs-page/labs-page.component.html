Labs module

The Labs Module provides healthcare providers with a comprehensive platform to manage lab tests, offering detailed information on test types, prescribing physicians, results, and recommended testing intervals. By offering a complete history of patient lab results, the module enables data-driven decision-making and enhances overall patient care.

Laboratory data is a critical resource in optimizing population health management and achieving Value-Based Care objectives. Virtu-Lab reports empower healthcare organizations and systems to identify high-risk patients and develop more effective clinical strategies.

By leveraging this trusted source of lab data, Virtu’s Labs Module enhances healthcare delivery through its comprehensive reporting and analytics platform, designed to pinpoint high-risk patients and improve clinical outcomes.

Main benefits

  • • Optimize Population Health Management: Leverage lab data to effectively manage high-risk populations, including patients with conditions like diabetes, chronic kidney disease, and cardiovascular disease.

  • • Access Comprehensive Patient Data: View a complete history of lab results to make informed decisions that improve patient care by analyzing both past and present test outcomes.

  • • Align with Value-Based Care Goals: Use lab data to enhance patient care strategies, supporting the achievement of Value-Based Care objectives.

  • • Minimize Duplicate Testing: Detect unnecessary duplicate tests and make adjustments following national guidelines, reducing costs and improving efficiency.
